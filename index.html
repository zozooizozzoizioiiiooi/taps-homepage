taps is a simple game where you compete for the speed of tapping buttons.

When you start the game, you will see multiple buttons on the screen. One of these buttons will light up, so tap that button. (In taps Ï€, buttons light up in the order of pi 3.141692... . In taps 4 and taps 9, buttons light up randomly.) If you can tap the lit button correctly, the next button will illuminate. Compete for the clear time until you tap the lit button 20 times. If you tap a button that is not lit, a penalty of +0.2 second will be added to the clear time.
